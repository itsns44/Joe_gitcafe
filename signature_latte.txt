#Signature Latte Recipe

##Ingredients:
1 shot of espresso (about 1.5 ounces)
8 ounces of steamed milk (whole milk or your preferred milk alternative)
1 tablespoon of flavored syrup (such as vanilla, caramel, hazelnut, etc.)
Whipped cream (optional, for topping)
Ground cinnamon or cocoa powder (optional, for garnish)
Instructions:
Prepare the Espresso:

Brew a shot of espresso using an espresso machine. It should yield about 1.5 ounces of espresso.
Steam the Milk:

Steam 8 ounces of milk using a milk steamer or frother until it reaches a creamy and frothy consistency.
Combine Espresso and Syrup:

Pour the shot of espresso into a large mug or glass.
Add 1 tablespoon of flavored syrup (adjust to taste preferences).
Pour Steamed Milk:

Slowly pour the steamed milk into the mug over the espresso and syrup mixture. Use a spoon to hold back the froth while pouring, then top with the froth at the end.
Top with Optional Whipped Cream:

If desired, add a dollop of whipped cream on top of the latte.
Garnish:

Sprinkle ground cinnamon or cocoa powder on top of the whipped cream for an extra touch (optional).
Serve Immediately:

Your signature latte is now ready to be served! Enjoy it while hot.
